About Sanjalika

Sanjalika Water Park is the ultimate destination for entertainment and adventure! Decorated with beautiful mermaids, stunning statues, and modern technology, our park features the largest cave and swimming pool area designed for thrills and family fun.

Experience an array of exciting and thrilling water rides that will make your visit unforgettable. From adrenaline-pumping slides to relaxing wave pools, we have something for everyone!
